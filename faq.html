FAQ

Is this free?

Yes. The stable release is free to use.

Is data private?

Yes. All settings are stored locally on your device.

Does it use internet?

Core features are fully offline*. Utilities (QR and QuickLink) are online-assisted.

Is it safe?

Yes. The app is lightweight and does not collect any personal data.

Future paid plans?

No paid plans are announced. Any future plans will be optional and transparent.
