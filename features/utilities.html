Utilities

Helpful tools when you need them

Utilities live inside the app and are optional. They assist with QR tasks and QuickLink shortening when you are online.

  • QR generation with customization
  • QR scanning for links and data
  • QuickLink URL shortening

* Some utility tools (QR generation, QR scanning, QuickLink shortening) require an internet connection. Core app features work fully offline*.
