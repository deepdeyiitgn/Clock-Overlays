About this app

Transparent Clock / Clock Overlays keeps a minimal, always-on-top clock visible while a calm dashboard provides Pomodoro sessions, To-Do tracking, Focus Insights, and utilities. It reduces context switching and keeps attention on the task at hand.

Transparent Clock was built to give focused users a simple way to keep time visible without opening distracting apps or widgets. The goal is to keep attention on study and deep work while still offering practical tools like a dashboard and a Pomodoro timer.

Design philosophy: minimal UI, distraction-free controls, and offline-by-default behavior.
